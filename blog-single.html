Successfully Trained MobileNetV2 Model

June 29, 2025

After preparing and augmenting our fruit and vegetable image dataset, we successfully trained a MobileNetV2 transfer learning model to classify fresh vs rotten images with over 93% test accuracy.

MobileNetV2 was chosen for its lightweight architecture, ideal for deployment on edge devices and mobile platforms. The model was trained using Keras, and the data was loaded via `ImageDataGenerator`.

Next steps include converting the model to TensorFlow Lite and integrating it into a web-based and mobile-friendly interface.
