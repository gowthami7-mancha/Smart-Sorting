Insights & Updates from the Development Journey

June 29, 2025

Successfully Trained MobileNetV2 Model

Today, we achieved over 93% accuracy in identifying rotten vs. fresh fruits using a pre-trained MobileNetV2 model. It was exciting to see the model perform so well after just 10 epochs!

June 27, 2025

Collected and Cleaned Image Dataset

Collected images of fresh and rotten fruits from public datasets and manually sorted them into categories. Created a structured dataset for training and testing. Also augmented the images using Keras generators.

June 25, 2025

Project Kickoff: Smart Sorting

Started the Smart Sorting project as part of the AI & ML internship. The goal is to classify fruit/vegetable images as fresh or rotten using deep learning and deploy it via a web interface. Excited to begin!
